ID: ed314256 The most recent iteration of the immersive theater experience Sleep No More, which premiered in New York City in 2011, transforms its performance space-a five-story warehouse-into a 1930s-era hotel. Audience members, who wander through the labyrinthine venue at their own pace and follow the actors as they play out simultaneous, interweaving narrative loops, confront the impossibility of experiencing the production in its entirety.
